# Xiaohongshu audit reference

Last checked: 2026-07-17. Treat official rules as hard gates and creative patterns as hypotheses.

## Current rule findings

- Community Covenant 2.0 (2026-01-19) emphasizes truthful sharing and friendly interaction; opposes manufactured group conflict, false personas, online violence, false marketing, and harm to minors; asks creators using AI assistance to mark it proactively.
- Official topic rules prohibit false or exaggerated claims, privacy violations, emotional incitement, imitation, advertising, and begging for likes/follows/saves. Because this is a topic-rule page, do not misrepresent it as the entire note policy.
- The Pugongying content review standard, updated 2026-06-12, governs collaboration notes and adds a stricter commercial review layer. It rejects obfuscation and low-information content. Apply commercial disclosure and quality requirements when the draft is sponsored; do not impose the collaboration workflow on an ordinary note.
- Platform rules are dynamic. Verify the current publication UI for AI labels and commercial disclosures.

## Audit interpretation

- `搜索、收藏、主页访问、具体问题` are diagnostic measures, not promises of distribution.
- A save-worthy object is a usable step, checklist, comparison, template, or reference—not a command to save.
- Search phrasing must remain natural. Repeating keywords without adding meaning is a platform-fit defect.
- The title must be fully earned by the body. Remove numbers, universals, and causal language that the proof cannot support.
- Recent May 2026 statements about saves, follows, long watch time, and 90-day recommendation life concerned a medium/long-video program; do not generalize them to all Xiaohongshu posts.
- Creator/teacher statements about algorithms, viral standards, income, and thresholds remain attributed experience or interpretation unless current primary evidence verifies them.
- Audit with `same demand, different evidence`: retain a useful audience problem, not another creator's title, wording, personal story, data, screenshots, or information sequence. Synonym replacement does not make a copy original.

## Evidence language

Prefer `本次记录`, `在这个任务里`, `目前样本`, `我的判断`, and `仍需发布验证`. Reject `一定`, `人人都`, `平台最喜欢`, `必爆`, or an exact benchmark without a source.
