# Xiaohongshu platform rules and method

Last checked: 2026-07-17. Recheck because platform rules are dynamic.

## Evidence labels

- `Official rule`: a compliance boundary. Treat as a hard gate.
- `Official product signal`: applies only to the named format or program.
- `Public sample`: supports a creative hypothesis, not an algorithm claim.
- `Account result`: only the creator's own publication data can validate performance.
- `Creator/teacher source claim`: attributed experience or interpretation. It may suggest a test, but it is not an official ranking rule, universal viral standard, or verified income result.

## Current rule findings

1. Xiaohongshu Community Covenant 2.0, released 2026-01-19, retains `真诚分享、友好互动` and adds explicit opposition to manufactured group conflict, false personas, online violence, false marketing, and harm to minors. It asks creators using AI assistance to mark it proactively.
2. The official topic rules encourage content grounded in personal experience and prohibit false or exaggerated claims, privacy violations, emotional incitement, imitation, advertising, and begging for likes/follows/saves. This is a topic-rule page, so use it as narrow evidence rather than claiming every line is the complete note policy.
3. The official Pugongying content review standard, updated 2026-06-12, applies to commercial collaboration notes. It adds a stricter commercial layer: safety, content, and client review; no obfuscation through misspellings or homophones; low-information motivational content can be rejected. Do not apply commercial-only workflow to ordinary notes, but keep its quality and disclosure principles when content is sponsored.
4. The current Pugongying help center lists Community Covenant 2.0, legal requirements for collaboration notes, and governance notices against false and low-quality marketing.

## Recent official/public signals

- At Xiaohongshu's May 2026 creator event, platform speakers described saves, follows, long watch time, horizontal viewing, and danmu as signals for the new medium/long-video program and said recommendation life could extend to 90 days. Do not generalize this to every image-text note or promise distribution.
- Logged-in keyword snapshots are personalized and time-sensitive. Treat their counts and result order as point-in-time samples, not universal rankings.

## Method hypotheses to validate

- Search-first packaging works when the post directly answers a recognizable question.
- Saves are more meaningful when the user receives a reusable step, checklist, comparison, or template.
- Profile visits and specific questions are stronger positioning signals than generic likes.
- Strong titles must remain fully earned by the body. Numbers should come from dated evidence, not copywriting convenience.

## Operational boundary

Do not claim an official ranking formula, universal best length, guaranteed traffic, or a fixed keyword density. Ask for the account's own search-entry, save, profile-visit, follow, and qualified-question data after publication.

When learning from a public hit, use `same demand, different evidence`: do not copy its title, distinctive wording, personal story, data, screenshots, or information sequence. Synonym replacement is not an originality method.
