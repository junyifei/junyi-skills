# WeChat Channels audit reference

Last checked: 2026-07-17. Official rules are dynamic; public creative patterns are hypotheses.

## Current official rule findings

The current `微信视频号运营规范` requires truthful, safe use and addresses:

- False identity/data, deceptive content, false publicity, undisclosed advertising, and fraud.
- Rewarded, coerced, threatening, or inflammatory engagement requests.
- Exaggerated titles, title/content mismatch, fabricated sensational stories, fake misery, rapid-income myths, and emotional incitement.
- Copyright, portrait, privacy, personal-information, and business-secret violations.
- Plagiarism, moving/reposting, and other intellectual-property infringement. A synonym-level paraphrase of another creator's story or shot sequence is not an original work method.
- Minors' privacy and wellbeing; caution and rights-holder consent for a minor's name, likeness, voice, or behavior.
- Near-identical fabricated batches and low-quality text slideshows, unclear video, unrelated audio/visuals, misleading edits, or stale events.
- Prominent labeling of AI-generated/synthetic non-real audio/video and fictionalized performance.

Official source: https://weixin.qq.com/cgi-bin/readtemplate?lang=zh_CN&t=weixin_agreement&s=video

## Audit interpretation

- `前三秒人物与关系冲突` is a creative method, not a published official ranking rule.
- `转给谁` should identify a relevant relationship; it must not reward, threaten, shame, or force engagement.
- Completion, shares, relationship comments, follows, and consultations diagnose different content jobs. Never invent target rates.
- A real event can end with mixed evidence. Do not force success, warmth, or closure.
- Creator/teacher statements about algorithms, viral standards, revenue, and thresholds are attributed experience or interpretation, not official platform facts. Use them only to form a test unless independently verified.
- Audit with `same demand, different evidence`: preserve the audience problem if useful, but require the current author's own event, proof, judgment, language, and shot order.
- If the current creator backend or publication UI differs from this reference, the official live UI and current rule text take precedence.
